Volunteer at Premissa

Premissa is a non-profit community space in Ponta Delgada dedicated to creative freedom, social inclusion, and well-being through art, physical activity, and connection.

We are always grateful for volunteers who offer their time and energy — whether it’s helping at events, supporting workshops, sharing knowledge, or simply being present with others.

As a thank-you, we offer free access to our spaces and activities, and can provide Bescheinigungen oder Zeugnisse (official certificates or letters of reference) for your voluntary contribution.
