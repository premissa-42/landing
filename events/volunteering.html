Volunteer at Premissa

Premissa is a non-profit community space in Ponta Delgada dedicated to creativity, inclusion, and well-being through art, activity, and exchange.

We’re always grateful for volunteers who offer their time — whether by helping during events, assisting workshops, or just being supportive in our shared space.

You’ll receive free access to our programs and, if requested, certificates or letters of reference confirming your voluntary contribution.
