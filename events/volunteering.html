VOLUNTEER AT PREMISSA

Support community, creativity & care — and receive recognition.

Premissa is a non-profit community center in Ponta Delgada dedicated to creativity, inclusion, and collective well-being — through art, activity, and connection.

We’re always grateful for volunteers who offer their time — helping at events, supporting workshops, sharing knowledge, or simply showing up in presence and care.

Volunteers enjoy free access to all our programs and activities, and we’re happy to provide Bescheinigungen oder Zeugnisse (certificates or letters of reference) for your contribution to the community.
