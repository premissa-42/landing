Volunteer at Premissa

Be part of something meaningful.

Premissa is a non-profit community center in Ponta Delgada (Azores), fostering creativity, well-being, and connection through arts, physical activities, and inclusive events.

We welcome all who want to help — whether by supporting workshops, assisting during events, or simply sharing skills and time with others.

Volunteers enjoy free access to our spaces and activities, and we are happy to issue Bescheinigungen oder Zeugnisse (official confirmation letters or certificates) acknowledging your voluntary engagement.

Ready to join us or curious to learn more? Reach out via premissahibrida@gmail.com or drop by during our open hours.
