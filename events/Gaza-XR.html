Gaza XR

13 September 2025 · 17:00–22:00
At 42 Premissa, Rua do Laureano 200/11, Ponta Delgada

Gaza XR is a multi-person VR exhibit created by Palestinian artists, journalists and researchers. Step into Gaza’s streets, homes and landmarks through immersive 360° footage – from everyday life to its destruction.

A quiet, guided space for reflection, learning and solidarity. Free entry – drop in anytime during the session.
