Bi Visibility Exhibition

photography · identity · bi fest 2025

7 September 2025
At Premissa Hybrid Space, Rua do Laureano 200/11, Ponta Delgada

The Bialogar Podcast, in collaboration with As Cores dos Açores – Opus Diversidades, presented an exhibition dedicated to Bisexual Visibility at Premissa.

The show brought together photographs of bisexual people – both famous and anonymous – as well as portraits and artistic representations celebrating the diversity and plurality of Bi identities.

This event is part of Bi Fest 2025, a national set of activities taking place throughout September to amplify bisexual voices and visibility.

The exhibition remains open until the end of September — visit anytime.
