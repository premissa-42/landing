PAA - Premissa Açores Associação

A cultural and social center for art, movement, and community. Located in Ponta Delgada, São Miguel.

Who We Are

Premissa is a non-profit association based in the Azores. Since 2023, we’ve worked to create inclusive, accessible spaces for artistic expression, physical activity, and community building. Our home, Premissa Híbrida, hosts workshops, residencies, events, and informal gatherings throughout the year.

Our focus is local — and intersectional. We prioritize queer, trans, migrant, and working-class communities, and collaborate with artists, educators, and cultural organizers across the islands and internationally.

Why We Exist

We believe spaces matter. Premissa exists to provide a physical and cultural environment where expression, community care, and creative work are possible — especially for those who are often excluded from institutional spaces.

Our vision is sustainable, inclusive, and grounded in the Azores.
