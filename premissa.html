Premissa Açores Associação

Social, artistic and inclusive — rooted in São Miguel, moving with light.

Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Premissa is a hybrid community space in Ponta Delgada, Azores. We focus on inclusive art practices, physical and emotional well-being, and community empowerment — with a special commitment to LGBTQ+ youth, creative expression, and cultural access.
