Premissa Açores Associação

Premissa is a hybrid, inclusive space founded in 2023 in Ponta Delgada, São Miguel (Azores), dedicated to social cohesion, youth engagement, artistic expression, and sustainability. We offer workshops, community events, VR exhibitions, and safe spaces for underrepresented groups—especially LGBTQ+ youth.

Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable and inclusive environment that promotes creativity, physical activity, and social interaction for all. We support vulnerable groups through accessible art, movement, education, and community engagement.
