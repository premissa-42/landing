Social Hours

Climbing, rest and shared time.

Bouldering, a small fitness area, coworking tables, sofas, games, a garden full of banana trees, a coffee machine, internet, books, free art supplies and a quiet VR corner.

Every Monday 18h-21h.

What we offer

Bouldering Indoor bouldering wall in São Miguel in the Azores with chalk and shoes provided. Donation for drop-ins: 3–5€.
Training area Basic equipment for strength, warm up and mobility.
Coworking & internet Tables, outlets and a coffee machine.
Games & art corner Board and card games plus space for drawing and small creative projects.
Banana garden Outdoor area surrounded by banana trees for fresh air and small breaks.
Books Small lending shelf for reading on site or taking home.
Art & craft supplies Free materials to experiment with, from pens and paper to simple craft tools.
Yoga & pilates Regular classes with simple sign-up.
VR corner Low-light room for short VR sessions.
