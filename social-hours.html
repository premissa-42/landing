Social Hours & Climbing

Climbing, rest and shared time.

Bouldering, a small fitness area, coworking tables, sofas, games, a garden full of banana trees, a coffee machine, internet, books, free art supplies and a quiet VR corner.

What we offer

Bouldering Indoor wall in São Miguel with chalk provided. Donation for drop-ins: 3–5€.
Training area Basic equipment for strength, warm up and mobility.
Coworking & internet Tables, outlets and a coffee machine.
Games & banana garden Board games, card games and access to the garden surrounded by banana trees.
Yoga & pilates Regular classes with simple sign-up.
Books & art supplies Lending shelf and free materials for drawing and making things.
VR corner Cushioned, low-light room for short VR sessions.
