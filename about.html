About the Project

42 – A Virtual Museum is a digital extension of the Island Horizon initiative by PAA – Premissa Açores Associação. It’s a creative and participatory platform rooted in community, focused on memory, identity, and social inclusion.

Rather than functioning as a traditional museum, 42 is a dynamic digital space that brings together virtual exhibitions, stories, and workshops created by and with youth, especially those from LGBTQ+ and economically marginalized communities in the Azores. It bridges physical and digital geographies, using tools like Virtual Reality and the Photovoice methodology to explore lived experiences and collective imagination.

The platform features immersive cultural content developed through international partnerships, educational programs, and creative residencies — connecting local voices to global conversations.

Created by Premissa and supported by Jovens+ and Drac-RJAAC, the project is part gallery, part archive, and part workshop — an invitation to experience, co-create, and reflect.
