Premissa is where inclusion, culture, and community intersect.

About the Project

42 is a 60m² modular space inside the Premissa Community Center in Ponta Delgada, São Miguel (Azores). It’s equipped with digital tools like VR headsets and projection gear — and most activities are free, designed especially for youth and marginalized communities, with a focus on LGBTQ+ inclusion.

We host exhibitions, workshops, and immersive experiences — and bring global content to the island through open digital formats.

The project is run by PAA – Premissa Açores Associação, with support from Jovens+ and Drac-RJAAC.

Who We Are

PAA was founded in 2023 to create hybrid cultural and physical spaces that are welcoming to all. Our center includes areas for movement, reflection, creation, and gathering.

From yoga and beach clean-ups to digital art labs and queer archives, Premissa is a constellation of connection in São Miguel.
