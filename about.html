About the Project

42 – A Virtual Museum is an evolving digital space exploring memory, identity, and imagination through curated digital experiences. It's not quite a museum, and not quite not one — a hybrid realm between archives and dreams.

The project celebrates diverse voices and speculative futures, drawing from queer histories, island perspectives, and collective co-creation. It offers visitors an interactive experience where digital space becomes both gallery and playground.

42 is powered by curiosity, shaped by participation, and open to experimentation. It's designed to grow and shift — like memory itself.

Created by Premissa, and supported by Jovens+ and Drac-RJAAC, this space invites you to explore, reflect, and maybe even leave something behind.
