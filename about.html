About the Project

42 is a 60m² space inside the Premissa community center in Ponta Delgada, São Miguel (Azores). It's equipped with digital tools, including VR headsets and projection equipment.

Most activities are free and focus on young people and people from marginalized backgrounds, with special attention to LGBTQ+ youth.

42 hosts exhibitions, workshops, and virtual reality experiences, and brings international projects and artworks to the island.

The project is run by PAA – Premissa Açores Associação, with support from Jovens+ and Drac-RJAAC.
