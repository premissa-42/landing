The Nebula

A weekly space for LGBTQIA+ youth to gather, decompress, and just be.

What it is

Each week, 42 opens its doors for LGBTQIA+ youth to meet, hang out, and participate in informal programming. Sometimes it's a workshop or a themed session. Sometimes it's just a soft room with tea and space to be together.

What happens here

Social drop-ins
Peer support & conversations
Gender expression & care labs
Media nights, games, food
Quiet corners & creative time
Space to feel seen

Why it matters

This isn’t a program. It’s protection, possibility, presence.
A space to exist without explanation.
A weekly rhythm of queer joy and rest — held gently, together.
